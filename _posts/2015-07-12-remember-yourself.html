--- layout: post title: "Remember yourself" date: 2015-07-12 21:54:03 ---

Just a personal reminder
that might be applicable
to other people too:

Remember yourself.

– if you are very empathic,
it might happen that you feel
other persons feelings
as if they were your own.
This is both good and bad.

It allows you to connect
and see the world
from another side.

But it also has the danger
of loosing oneself.

Therefore it is important,
to stop up and feel,
what comes from the environment
and what comes from yourself.
Remember what you want,
and do not just follow the flow.

Be more selfish,
such that you do
what is important
for your-self.
