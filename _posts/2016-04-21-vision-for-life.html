--- layout: post title: "Vision for life" date: 2016-04-21 03:35:23 ---

Where will you be years from now?
What is your direction?

The goal that I am moving towards is
a life style of creating and learning,
centred around short projects.

Mastering going from idea to initial execution
in a matter of days or weeks.

Walking into a contexts,
quickly deliver good solutions,
and then continue onwards,
focusing on the iteration of creation.

These months I work intensely
on a couple of larger projects,
which have the effect of improving
my toolbox for rapid development
as well as giving me an economic buffer,
finishing the foundation I will build upon.

This paves the path for my journey,
going further towards the archetype
of the wandering wizard.
