Is the focus narrow or wide?

When you look at the different things you do,
it is sometimes good to be conscious about
whether you choose a wide or narrow focus.

Given the same time,
you can acquire high expertise in a detail,
basic skills in general.High expertise is often more valued,
as this is hard to come by,
while general skills may give more growth,
as the rate of improvement is higher.Often it is also best
to focus on improving the area
where you are the weakest.
