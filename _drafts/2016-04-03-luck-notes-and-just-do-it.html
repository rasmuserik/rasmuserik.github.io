--- layout: post title: "Luck, notes, and just do it" date: 2016-04-03 08:03:24 ---

Sometimes I listen to audio books, when doing various stuff. Recently I heard “The Virgin Way”, and three points that I felt was good to be reminded of was:

  • “Luck is what happens when preparation meets opportunity,” – which is actually a quote from Seneca, which is very worthwhile to remember. This also reminds me that I want to look more into Seneca.
  • “Screw it, let’s do it” – a recurrent mantra in the book, which has a good attitude.
  • Taking notes – which reminds me, to try to bring a notebook along, so I can capture ideas etc., and to try to make it a daily habit to empty it into an searchable/electronic form in my general note system.
